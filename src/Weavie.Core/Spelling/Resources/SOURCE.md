# Spelling resources

The `en_*.aff` and `en_*.dic` files are unmodified copies from LibreOffice
`dictionaries` commit `208a9fd80b2a182fe20f224cd615119c6323ae2e`, directory
[`en/`](https://github.com/LibreOffice/dictionaries/tree/208a9fd80b2a182fe20f224cd615119c6323ae2e/en).

The upstream attribution and license notice is preserved verbatim at
`third-party/spelling/LibreOffice-English-README.txt` and ships with the distribution.

The `CSpell/` assets are unmodified copies from Street Side Software's
[`cspell-dicts` commit `83dbd80ba3b0e76b875534da7fa5f7d313ef5ea5`](https://github.com/streetsidesoftware/cspell-dicts/tree/83dbd80ba3b0e76b875534da7fa5f7d313ef5ea5).
Its MIT license is preserved verbatim at `third-party/spelling/cspell-dicts-LICENSE.txt` and ships
with the distribution.
